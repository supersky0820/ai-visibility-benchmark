AI Search Visibility of 101 SaaS Brands — Measured on Perplexity

We asked an AI answer engine about 101 SaaS brands across 23 categories — the brand itself, its reviews, and its alternatives — and recorded which sources it actually cited. This is the anonymous, aggregate picture of AI search visibility in mid-2026.

Anonymous by design: this benchmark publishes aggregate numbers only. No per-brand results are released and no brands are ranked — the point is how the AI recommendation mechanism behaves, not who wins.

02 · The gap

Being well-known is not enough. AI assembles alternatives answers from third-party comparison content — community threads, listicles, review platforms — and a brand that is absent from those surfaces is absent from the answer, no matter how big its name is.

brands% vs share% — two lenses, not interchangeable

brands% = coverage breadth: the number of brands (out of 101) for which a source was cited at least once, ÷ 101. Derived from the raw count in sources.top[].brands.

share% = citation share: a source's citations ÷ all citations. Stored directly in sources.top[].share.

06 · Methodology

Methodology

Honesty rules: failed calls are recorded as unknown and excluded from every denominator — never counted as "not cited". In this run, all brands completed all three queries.
